    Number Filters
    • Number Filter : {{1234567890 | number}}
    • Decimal (round 2): {{1234.5678 | number:2}}

    Currency Filter
    • Currency : {{123 | currency}}

    Date Filters
    • Date (medium) : {{clock.now | date:'medium'}}
    • Date (short) : {{clock.now | date:'short'}}
    • Date (fullDate) : {{clock.now | date:'fullDate'}}
    • Date (longDate) : {{clock.now | date:'longDate'}}
    • Date (mediumDate) : {{clock.now | date:'mediumDate'}}
    • Date (shortDate) : {{clock.now | date:'shortDate'}}
    • Date (mediumTime) : {{clock.now | date:'mediumTime'}}
    • Date (shortTime) : {{clock.now | date:'shortTime'}}

    Year Filter Formatting
    • Four digit year : {{clock.now | date:'yyyy'}}
    • Two digit year : {{clock.now | date:'yy'}}
    • One digit year : {{clock.now | date:'y'}}

    Month Filter Formatting
    • Month Full: {{clock.now | date:'MMMM'}}
    • Month Short: {{clock.now | date:'MMM'}}
    • Month in year: (0 padding): {{clock.now | date:'MM'}}
    • Month in year: {{clock.now | date:'M'}}

    Day Filter Formatting
    • Padded day in month: {{clock.now | date:'dd'}}
    • Day in month: {{clock.now | date:'d'}}
    • Day in week: {{clock.now | date:'EEEE'}}
    • Short day in week: {{clock.now | date:'EEE'}}

    Hour Filter Formatting
    • Padded hour in day: {{clock.now | date:'HH'}}
    • Hour in day: {{clock.now | date:'H'}}
    • Padded hour in am/pm: {{clock.now | date:'hh'}}
    • Hour in am/pm: {{clock.now | date:'h'}}

    Minute Filter Formatting
    • Padded minute in hour: {{clock.now | date:'mm'}}
    • Minute in hour: {{clock.now | date:'m'}}

    Second Filter Formatting
    • Padded second in minute: {{clock.now | date:'ss'}}
    • Second in minute: {{clock.now | date:'s'}}

    Custom Filters for date
    • MMM d, y => {{clock.now | date:'MMM d, y'}}
    • MMMM dd yyyy, HH mm ss => {{clock.now | date:'MMMM dd yyyy, HH mm ss'}}
